Game Rules

How to Play Zero to 100

Objective

Test your knowledge by guessing numerical answers to various questions. The closer your guess is to the correct answer, the better your score will be!

Gameplay

  1. You will be presented with 10 random questions.
  2. Each question has a numerical answer between 0 and 100.
  3. Type your best guess in the input field.
  4. Click "Submit Guess" to see the correct answer.
  5. Your score for each question is the absolute difference between your guess and the correct answer.
  6. For example, if the correct answer is 75 and you guess 70, your score is 5 points.
  7. Click "Next Question" to proceed to the next question.
  8. After 10 questions, your final score will be displayed.

Scoring

The goal is to achieve the lowest possible score. A perfect score would be 0, meaning you guessed all answers correctly!

Your final score is the sum of all differences across the 10 questions.

Tips

  • Remember that all answers are between 0 and 100.
  • Use your general knowledge to make educated guesses.
  • Try to remember common facts and statistics.
  • Have fun and challenge your friends to beat your score!
