Game Rules:

  • Each question has an answer between 0 and 100.
  • Enter your best guess for each question.
  • Your score for each question is the difference between your guess and the correct answer.
  • The game consists of 10 questions, and the final score is the total of all differences.
  • Try to get the lowest possible score!
  • If you want to return to this page while playing click on the title.
  • HAVE FUN!
